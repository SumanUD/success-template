Success Story

Success is an independent menswear brand built from the grassroots up by people who love fashion and all that it stands for. Established in 1996 in Kolkata by Mr. Rajnish Sethia, Success has been a part of men's fashion in Kolkata for over 26 years. Starting as a trouser manufacturer, we now offer full wardrobes—suits, blazers, shirts, trousers, denims, and accessories across 450+ outlets.
